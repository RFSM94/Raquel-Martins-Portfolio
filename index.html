ABOUT

I'm passionate about learning, spreading and contributing to inclusive and accessible information, all while influencing real community impact. I’m currently at the Portuguese Government but I’ve also worked in-house for media outlets like Observador and Eco and as a freelancer for several other smaller brands and producers, such as Digital Azul. With a proactive approach, I thrive in collaborative environments but am just as self-motivated accomplishing things on my own as a freelancer helping small brands get on their feet. All my past work experiences have taught me that the best creative work happens somewhere between a thoughtful strategy and a light-hearted outlook.
