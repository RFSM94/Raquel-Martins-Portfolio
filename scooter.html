Client: Observador
Role: Creative Direction, Visual Design, Motion Design

The scooter trend took off in Portugal, causing some confusion and hassle. Suddenly, scooters were everywhere, seen more as toys than a serious mode of transportation. To help clear things up, together with a journalist we created a video guide on safely navigating the city on a scooter. Our main goal? Make the rules simple and enjoyable to follow. As the creative lead, I collaborated with the journalist to storyboard the video, creating all the visuals and animating the data. Originally, it was considered using stop motion, but due to time constraints, we opted for a digital approach, adding paper textures for a bit of flair.

We aimed for inclusivity, so I designed a gender-neutral character. The shapes and icons we used were straightforward and playful, with a muted color palette for universal appeal.
